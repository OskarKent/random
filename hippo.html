fun facts about hippos

  • hippos can not swim or float
  • hippos can bounce off the bottom of the water to come up
  • hippos can hold their breath for up to 5 minutes
  • a group of hippos is called a dale, herd, pod, or bloat
  • some hippos can run up to 30 kilometers per hour
  • hippos can sleep underwater
  • hippopotamus means river horse
  • a hippo has a bite force of about 2,000 pounds per square inch
  • a hippo can not jump on land
